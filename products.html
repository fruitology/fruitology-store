About Nak Fazli Mango

Nak Fazli is one of Bangladesh's most beloved late-season mangoes, grown primarily in the Chapai Nawabganj and Rajshahi regions. Known for its large size, golden-orange flesh, and a perfectly balanced sweet-tangy taste with rich aroma — it is prized for eating fresh. Unlike artificially ripened mangoes found in markets, our Nak Fazli mangoes ripen entirely on the tree, resulting in natural sweetness, better nutrition, and no harmful residues.
